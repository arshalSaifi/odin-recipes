Naan

Description

Naan is a central asian bread which made its way to the indian subcontinent.indians have reinvented their own version of naan which is thinner in comparison to the central asian naan

Ingredients

  • maida
  • egg
  • yeast
  1. add egg to maida
  2. make it a dough
  3. add yeast to the dough
  4. wait for 3-4 hours
  5. the dough is ready
